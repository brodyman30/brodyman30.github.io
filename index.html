About

Hello! My name is Brody Zwiebel, I'm a developer & designer

I'm a Kansas State Graduate, receiving my B.A.S in Advertising and Public Relations, with a Specialization in Digital and Social Media. I'm passionate about creating beautiful, functional web experiences. With a background in radio and graphic design, I bring a tech-centered approach to every project.

I specialize in front-end development, interactive design, and building tools that solve real problems. My work combines creative solutions with intuitive user interfaces. When I'm not exploring new tech you'll find me exploring music APIs, gaming, or working on creative multimedia projects.
